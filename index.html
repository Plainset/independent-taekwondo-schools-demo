About the association

A friendly, family club that's been training Aberdeenshire for years.

Independent Taekwondo Schools runs regular mixed-grade classes for children and adults, with monthly training seminars and grading tests supervised by Senior Master Murdoch, 8th Dan Black Belt. The club's membership is registered with Aberdeenshire ClubSport SCIO for 2024/25, and clubs across the area are currently taking on new students.

  • Free taster class for every new student — just email, phone, or message the club's Facebook page.
  • Instructors are PVG checked, First Aid qualified and Governing Body certified.
  • Regular monthly seminar and grading days, with Grading Trophy and Grading Shield awards for top students.
